About the Band

The Bad Ragas began in Zurich's rock scene, where high-energy live shows carried them to the Swiss finals of the Emergenza competition.

Now based in the UK, the band has evolved into a stripped-back but dynamic trio: Andy (vocals, guitar), Sarah (vocals, baritone ukulele), and Ray (violin). Their sound blends soulful originals like "Reload" with inventive covers that reshape familiar songs in unexpected ways.
